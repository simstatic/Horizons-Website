Our Story

The SnowBot was created by a team of four proactive Materials Scientists at Imperial College. Two of our members who are Polish reflected on the daily, inconvenient and strenuousness of removing snow from the tops of their cars, before they could drive anywhere in Winter in Poland. We discovered that across this was problem across Europe and many other parts of the world (although not a significant problem in the UK) due driving safety laws. After discovering that no automated snow-removing solution currently exists, the team decided to set about designing and prototyping the worldâ€™s first robot which could be used by people of all ages and abilities to start their day in a hassle free way. Special prominence was placed on making the device easy use, store and operate, to make it truly accessible for those with reduce mobility and or limited motion.

Snow Bot's Features

How our product will make your life easier!

Sticks to your Car!

Magnetic tracks to grip the robot to car rooftops

Removes Snow Quickly from your Car

Silicon lined snow plow to push snow across the surface of the car without scratching the car paintwork

Portable

Battery powered with rechargeable batteries (batteries must be recharged separately)

Durable

Robust steel bodied frame housing electrical components & circuits

Fully Automated

Sensors to detect the age of the car and create the optimum snow clearing path
